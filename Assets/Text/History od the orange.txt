DigitaruCamera Corporation is an organization of vegetable primer.
Created by James L. Labreum, with the goal of feeding all of us, they extended their activities to fruit and especially to oranges.
Over the years, the entire population bought their products.
Until the day when a decay orange, slipped into sales. Carrier of the virus O, it contaminated the whole package. Then this package contaminated a family, and then a building...
To save the Earth and the population, all human have been cryogenized and robots have been sent to exterminate all these contaminates, save the world from this parasite and finally defrost human to leave them in peace on the blue orange.
